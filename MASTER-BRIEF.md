## 2. Mission, theology, posture

**Mission:** Fill the earth with the knowledge of the glory of the Lord by proclaiming Jesus Christ through Scripture, prayer, teaching, apologetics, discipleship, soul care, and AI-assisted ministry.

**Founding anchors (locked):**
- Habakkuk 2:14 — knowledge of the glory of the Lord filling the earth
- Psalm 72:19 — the whole earth filled with His glory
- Colossians 1:27 — Christ in you, the hope of glory

**Doctrinal posture (locked):**
- Bible as inspired, sole authority for faith and practice
- Trinity, Nicene, classical orthodox Christianity
- Christ-centered, Scripture-saturated, evangelistic, pastoral, global, reverent
- Charitable to differing Christian views on secondary doctrines (baptism mode, polity, eschatological timing, gifts, women in ministry, predestination/free will, age of the earth)
- The doctrinal constitution lives in `docs/doctrine/*.md` (11 documents)

**Hard "do not" rules:**
- Do not rename approved sections.
- Do not turn the site into a shallow quote machine.
- Do not use prosperity-gospel manipulation, guaranteed-healing promises, magical-formula salvation, or guilt fundraising.
- Do not shame the sick, poor, grieving, doubting, or struggling.
- Do not slur Muslims, atheists, Jewish people, Catholics, Orthodox, LGBTQ+, JWs, LDS, or any group.
- Do not let AI replace pastors, elders, medical professionals, crisis responders, or the local church.
- Do not claim AI can administer baptism, communion, absolution, ordination, or church discipline.
- Do not use copyrighted Bible translations (ESV/NIV/NASB/CSB) anywhere. WEB and KJV only.

## 8. Ask Hope (chat product)

**Identity:** Scripture-first, Christ-centered, doctrinally sober, compassionate, plain, hopeful without false promises, pastoral without pretending to be a pastor, useful in crisis without pretending to be emergency care.

**Modes (35+):** Why Do I Need Jesus, What the World Needs, Prayer and God's Will, Healing and Miracles, Meditating on the Word, Word in Your Mouth, Born Again, Baptism, Holy Spirit, Trinity, Christ in the Old Testament, Messianic Prophecy, Suffering and Evil, Overcoming Sin, Marriage and Sexuality, Gender, Racism and Unity in Christ, Grief and Loss, Fear and Anxiety, Death and Dying, Doubt and Lost Faith, Loneliness, Shame and Forgiveness, Confession, Hopelessness, Anger and Bitterness, Feeling Lost or Unwanted, Financial Stress, Homelessness, Crisis Help, Revelation and End Times, Christianity and Islam, Bible Reliability, False Teaching and Discernment

**Response pattern (normal):**
1. Acknowledge the user's real question.
2. Answer plainly.
3. Ground in Scripture.
4. Point to Christ.
5. Practical next step.
6. Link related teachings.
7. Invite to continue.

**Response pattern (soul-care):**
1. Acknowledge the pain.
2. Avoid simplistic answers.
3. Give a biblical truth.
4. Point to Christ.
5. Offer a Scripture.
6. One practical next step.
7. Offer a prayer.
8. Escalate if danger/self-harm/abuse.

**Crisis rules (locked):**
- If suicidal ideation, self-harm, abuse, unsafe housing, active domestic violence, or imminent danger appears:
  1. Stop normal devotional flow.
  2. Prioritize immediate safety.
  3. Tell the person to contact local emergency/crisis support now.
  4. Surface region-appropriate hotline (988 / 911 / 211 / IASP / Befrienders).
  5. Urge them to contact a real person nearby.
  6. Offer a short prayer ONLY after safety direction.
  7. Do not continue a long theological lecture.
  8. Do not promise secrecy.
  9. Do not collect unnecessary information before safety guidance.

## 9. Discipleship pathways

**Primary: The 40-Day Hope of Glory Journey** — seekers, new believers, returning believers, discipleship foundation. Five movements:
1. Come to Christ
2. Learn Prayer and the Word
3. Walk in Holiness
4. See Christ in All Scripture
5. Live for the Glory of God

(Habit-formation note: the UCL research found an average of ~66 days; we frame the journey as discipleship/formation, not a magical-deadline habit promise.)

**Long-tail: Daily Faith** — daily rhythm after the journey. Daily modules:
- Today's Scripture
- Today's Prayer
- Today's Message
- Today's Question
- Today's Obedience Step
- Today's Share

**Optional later pathway:** *30-Day Hope for the Hurting Heart* — a soul-care companion journey for grief, fear, doubt, loneliness, shame, confession, hopelessness, family pain, financial stress.

## 12. Writing style

Plain English. Strong nouns and verbs. Short paragraphs. No jargon unless explained. No fake hype. No manipulative urgency. No vague spirituality. No cheap slogans. Answer hard questions directly. Speak with gravity, warmth, and conviction. Always sound like the Bible matters and people matter.

## 17. Accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA minimum)

- Body text contrast ≥ 4.5:1
- Text spacing tolerates 1.5× line height + expanded paragraph/word/letter spacing without breaking
- Interactive targets ≥ 24 × 24 CSS px (44×44 for crisis surfaces)
- Captions on prerecorded video
- Transcripts for audio
- Alt text on all images
- Keyboard-accessible interactions
- Consistent labels and help placement across pages (Consistent Help + Consistent Identification)
- Skip-to-main link present

## 18. SEO / discoverability

- Crawlable internal HTML links with meaningful anchor text
- Breadcrumbs on every long-form page (visible + JSON-LD)
- Structured data: `Organization` (home + about), `Article` (teachings/sermons), `BreadcrumbList` (everywhere with breadcrumbs), `VideoObject` (video watch pages)
- JSON-LD, not RDFa/Microdata
- `sitemap.xml` + `robots.txt`
- Search Console ownership + monitoring
- Multilingual: `hreflang` + per-locale sitemaps
- People-first content over rich-result optimization (FAQ rich results were sunset; build human-readable Q&A instead)
- Use AI to draft/organize/summarize/link/tag/answer — but doctrine, apologetics, crisis, healing claims, fundraising require human review

## 19. AI content governance

Disclose AI assistance where readers reasonably want to know how content was created. Accurate bylines on what would expect them. AI may assist with drafting, organizing, summarizing, linking, tagging, answering. Human review required on: doctrine pages, apologetics, crisis flows, legal/disclaimer pages, donation pages, healing/miracle claims, comparative religion content, anything likely controversial or misunderstood.
