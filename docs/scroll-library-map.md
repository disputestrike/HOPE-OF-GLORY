# The Scroll Library Map

How the Scroll archive (≈101 study threads) is organized into a public,
Christ-centered biblical-theology and apologetics library — and the book
lineup drawn from it.

The center of gravity of the whole archive is one question: **Who is God, how
is Jesus connected to the identity of YHWH, and how does the whole Bible point
to Christ?** The map below turns the raw study threads (research notes) into a
structured public canon (finished teachings + books). The raw chats are the
research archive; they are not the final product.

---

## The eight content pillars

### 1. Who Is God? (foundation)
The identity, name, and nature of God; the Shema; divine plurality.
Threads: Isaiah 45 & Monotheism · Plurality in One God · God's plural nature ·
Shema meaning / word comparison · God's real name · Zephaniah 3 Jehovah ·
Exodus 3 fire · Angels bless the lads · Genesis 28:16 · Psalm 82.
→ **Book: _Hear, O Israel_** (published).

### 2. The Absolute Formula: "I Am He"
Jesus speaking the self-identification of YHWH (*ani hu* / *egō eimi*).
Threads: Jesus I Am / Not I Will Be · Philippians 2:6 · Jesus–Father one ·
Acts 2 Lord · Glory of God Moses/Jesus · Rev & OT white hair · Daniel 7 ·
Zechariah 12:10 (x2) · Isaiah 45.
→ **Book: _I Am He_** (published).

### 3. Christ Hidden in Plain Sight (largest pillar)
Jesus across the Old Testament — types, patterns, prophecy.
Threads: Seed vs seeds / Abraham's seed singular · Genesis 3:15 · Genesis 15 ·
Genesis 49:10 · Joseph as Christ type / Joseph's dream · David Goliath Golgotha ·
Moses & the tabernacle / promised land · Melchizedek · Azazel scapegoat /
baptism · wicked tenants · Ezekiel→NT · why Jesus a carpenter · transfiguration.
→ **Book: _Christ Hidden in Plain Sight_** (published).

### 4. The Cross: The Highest Price and the Greatest Love (evangelistic)
The atonement and the gospel.
Threads: Highest price / sale · God's abandonment of Jesus · blood in God's
plan · God reconciling through Christ · what Jesus meant by "finished" · who
saves · Barabbas · Jesus betrayal & bread · lost sheep · OT silver price.
→ **Book: _The Highest Price and the Greatest Love_** (published).

### 5. Christianity and Islam (apologetics — handle with care)
Threads: Isaiah 42 & Muhammad · Islamic views on God · semantic deception ·
Bible vs Quran spirit · Muhammad's position · prophets & obedience · Gehenna vs
Jahannam · Jude & Enoch · theological error in argument · signs of the hour.
→ **Proposed next book: _Read It in Context_** (rigorous, never hostile).

### 6. Deeper Reading (biblical context & patterns)
Threads: meaning of 40 · Mary & the gardener · Jesus writing in sand · spit &
Jewish law · Jewish day start / prayer direction · two Sabbaths · seat of Moses ·
ancient creation worldviews · weeping for Tammuz · Adam & Eve naming.
→ **Proposed: a teaching series or short book, _Deeper Reading_.**

### 7. Following Jesus (discipleship & leadership)
Threads: meditation & stillness · God's judgment on David · testing in the
wilderness · 1 Timothy 3 qualifications/overview · adultery law · two Israels ·
Matthew's audience.
→ Feeds the **40-Day Hope of Glory Journey** and the discipleship surfaces.

### 8. Research Desk / Holding Area (do NOT publish as-is)
- **Treating fibroids options** — remove from Scroll entirely (off-topic).
- George of B'eltan history — hold until source/purpose clarified.
- Greek and English issues — too vague without the thread; review.
- Meaning of Jesus' words — too broad; split before categorizing.
- Clarify son's name — review and retitle.
- Signs of the Hour — review carefully before publication.

## The flagship book set (deliberate reading order)

1. **Hear, O Israel** — who God is (the foundation).
2. **I Am He** — that Jesus is the "I am he" of Isaiah (the hinge).
3. **Christ Hidden in Plain Sight** — that the whole OT was pointing to Him.
4. **The Highest Price and the Greatest Love** — what He did on the cross → come.

Next candidates: **Read It in Context** (Christianity & Islam) and **Deeper
Reading** (biblical patterns). Standard target per book: ~7 chapters, full-length.

## Editorial rule (research → public)

Each public article/teaching follows one format:
1. The Question 2. The Simple Answer 3. Key Scriptures (WEB) 4. Read in Context
5. The Deeper Biblical Connection 6. How It Points to Christ 7. Common
Objections 8. What This Means for You 9. Related Teachings 10. Come to Christ.

Books are built freely (read online + downloadable PDF, no email wall),
consistent with the ministry's ethic: the gospel is free. All Scripture is the
World English Bible (public domain). We compare beliefs; we never demean
persons.
