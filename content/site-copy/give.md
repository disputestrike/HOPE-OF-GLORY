You will never be pressured here. Most of what we make is free, and it will stay that way.

## What your gift funds

- Daily Word publication and email delivery.
- Sermon writing, recording, and hosting.
- The Ask Hope Bible chat and the Hope Line phone service.
- Audio production and bandwidth.
- Website, AI tools, and basic operations.
- A small allowance for the people who do the work.

We will publish an annual summary of how funds were spent. If we ever cannot, we will say so plainly.

## Our giving ethics

We want to be the kind of ministry you would be glad to support and unembarrassed to recommend. So here is what we promise:

**No guilt.** We will not tell you God will withhold blessing if you do not give. We will not run countdown timers. We will not stage emergencies.

**No prosperity promises.** We will not promise that your gift will return to you as money, healing, or success. Giving is a gift, not a transaction.

**No pressure on the poor.** If money is tight, do not give. Pray for us and tell a friend about the ministry. That is worth more than a strained donation.

**No selling your data.** Donors are never sold, shared, or rented. See our [privacy policy](/privacy).

**No hidden compensation.** We will be honest about how staff are paid, in our annual summary.

**Modesty in salary.** Whoever leads this ministry will not be paid extravagantly. The Lord owns the cattle on a thousand hills. We do not need a private jet.

If that is you today — thank you. If it is not, please keep your money and keep reading. The gospel is still free.
