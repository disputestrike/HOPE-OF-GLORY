# Disclaimer

> What we offer, and what we don't.

---

Hope of Glory Ministry is a Christian media and discipleship ministry. We exist to
proclaim Jesus Christ through Scripture, prayer, teaching, apologetics, and AI-assisted
digital ministry.

This page is a summary of the limits on what we do. For more detail, see our
[Terms](/terms), [Privacy policy](/privacy), [AI disclosure](/ai-disclosure), and
[Crisis disclaimer](/crisis-disclaimer).

## We are not professional advisors

The teaching, articles, prayer prompts, and AI responses on this site are for general
biblical and spiritual education. They are not:

- Medical advice
- Mental health diagnosis or treatment
- Legal advice
- Financial advice
- Emergency response

If you need professional help, please consult a licensed professional in your area.

## We are not the local church

We are not your church. We do not administer baptism, the Lord's Supper, ordination,
membership, or church discipline. Those belong to embodied local congregations.
Every discipleship pathway on this site points you toward a faithful local church.

## Scripture quotations

Scripture quotations on this site are from the **World English Bible (WEB)**, which is
in the public domain, or the **King James Version (KJV)**, also in the public
domain — both freely shared so they may travel as far as they can. We do not
use copyrighted modern translations (ESV, NIV, NASB, CSB) anywhere on this site.

## AI-assisted content

Sermons, articles, prayer prompts, Daily Word entries, audio, visuals, and Ask Hope
chat responses on this site are produced with the help of AI tools. Human reviewers
oversee what we publish, but no review process is perfect. If you find an error —
factual, doctrinal, scriptural, or otherwise — please tell us, and we will correct it
on the [Corrections](/corrections) page.

See our [AI disclosure](/ai-disclosure) for more.

## No guarantee of outcomes

Christianity is honest. The Bible does not promise that every prayer will be answered
the way you want, that every illness will be healed in this life, that every marriage
will be saved, or that every Christian's life will be easy. We do not promise those
things either.

What Scripture does promise — what we will preach — is that Christ is with you, that
his grace is sufficient, that he is making all things new, and that nothing can
separate you from his love.

## External links

This site links to external resources (crisis hotlines, government services, church
directories, theological libraries, news, scholarship, and so on). We do not control
those sites and are not responsible for their content. We try to link only to
resources we believe are trustworthy, but please use your judgment.

This disclaimer may be updated as the ministry grows. Significant changes will be
