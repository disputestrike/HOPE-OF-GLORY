# How We Use AI

Hope of Glory is an AI-assisted Christian ministry. We want you to know exactly what that means before you read, listen, or write to us.

We believe technology is a tool. Used honestly, it lets a small team serve more people with the gospel of Jesus Christ. Used dishonestly, it can mislead. We are committed to honest.

---

## What AI helps us do

We use AI tools to help with:

- **Sermons and teaching.** AI helps draft outlines, study notes, and manuscripts. A human reviews and edits every published sermon for scriptural accuracy.
- **The Daily Word.** AI helps draft short reflections on scripture. A human reviews each one before it goes out.
- **Prayer prompts.** AI helps shape prayer language and scripture pairings. Your private prayer requests are read and prayed over by people, not bots.
- **Ask Hope chat.** The Bible Q&A chat is an AI assistant trained to point to scripture. Answers are generated in real time.
- **Audio.** Some of our spoken content is produced with AI voice tools. We will mark it when that is the case.
- **Visuals.** Some images on this site are AI-generated. We will not present AI images as real photographs of real events.

---

## What AI is not

We say this plainly so there is no confusion:

**AI is not a pastor.** It cannot shepherd you, baptize you, sit with you in suffering, or know your name before the Lord.

**AI is not a priest or spiritual director.** It cannot give absolution. It cannot replace confession to God or accountability with brothers and sisters in Christ.

**AI is not a counselor or therapist.** It cannot diagnose or treat mental health conditions.

**AI is not a doctor.** It cannot evaluate symptoms or give medical advice.

**AI is not a lawyer.** It cannot give legal advice.

**AI is not a prophet.** It does not speak new revelation. The canon of scripture is closed.

**AI is not an emergency responder.** It cannot send help.

---

## In a crisis, do not use this site

If you are in danger, in a mental health emergency, or thinking of harming yourself or someone else, please stop and get real help right now.

- **Medical or safety emergency:** call **911** (U.S.).
- **Suicide or crisis:** call or text **988** (U.S. Suicide and Crisis Lifeline).
- **Outside the U.S.:** please call your local emergency number.

Our [Hope Line](/hope-line) is a conversation and prayer line, not an emergency service. It is staffed only during posted hours.

---

## Human review

Every public-facing teaching piece on this site is reviewed by a human believer before publication. We make mistakes. When we do, we correct them on our [Corrections](/corrections) page.

If you spot an error — a misquoted verse, a doctrinal mistake, anything — please tell us. We would rather be corrected than be wrong.

---

## Your data and the AI

When you use the Ask Hope chat, your messages are processed by AI systems to generate a response. We do not sell, share, or publish your conversations. For full detail, see our [Privacy](/privacy) policy.

---

## Why we are doing it this way

We want the knowledge of the glory of the Lord to reach as far as it can, as faithfully as it can, for as many people as possible. AI helps us do that on a small budget without lowering the standard of truth.

We will keep telling you what we are doing and how. If our use of AI ever changes, this page will change with it.
