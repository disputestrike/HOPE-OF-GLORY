# Our Mission

Hope of Glory exists to fill the earth with the knowledge of the glory of the Lord — through scripture, sound teaching, prayer, and honest answers, made freely available wherever people can hear them.

---

## Three anchors

Our mission stands on three passages of scripture. We return to them often, because they keep us pointed at the Lord and not at ourselves.

### Habakkuk 2:14

> "For the earth will be filled with the knowledge of the glory of the Lord, as the waters cover the sea." (WEB)

This is a promise, not a strategy. The glory of God will fill the earth. Our part is small — to be one of the streams that helps carry that knowledge to one more heart, one more home, one more language, one more day.

We do not have to make it happen. We get to take part in what He is already doing.

### Psalm 72:19

> "Blessed be his glorious name forever! Let the whole earth be filled with his glory! Amen and amen." (WEB)

This is a prayer. It is also a posture. Everything we publish — every sermon, every prayer, every answer in the chat — is meant to bless His name, not ours.

If anything we make pulls attention to a personality, a platform, or a brand instead of to the Lord, we are doing it wrong, and we want to know.

### Colossians 1:27

> "...the riches of the glory of this mystery among the Gentiles, which is Christ in you, the hope of glory." (WEB)

Christ in you. That is the hope. Not Christ at a distance. Not Christ in theory. Christ dwelling in His people by His Spirit, transforming them from the inside out.

This is why we publish, why we pray, why we answer questions. So that more people will hear the gospel, believe, and find Christ Himself living in them.

---

## What we do

- Publish a daily scripture and short reflection.
- Teach the Bible, verse by verse, in written and audio form.
- Answer honest questions about scripture and the Christian faith.
- Pray for the people who write to us.
- Point seekers and new believers to faithful local churches.
- Offer a free phone line during set hours for conversation and prayer.

We use AI tools to help produce this work at a scale a small team could not otherwise reach. Every public-facing piece is reviewed by a human before it goes out. See our [AI disclosure](/ai-disclosure) for details.

---

## What we will not do

- We will not manipulate, guilt, or frighten you into giving.
- We will not promise health, wealth, or a problem-free life in exchange for faith.
- We will not set dates for the return of Christ.
- We will not pretend AI is a pastor, prophet, or counselor.
- We will not sell or share the information you trust us with.

Hope of Glory is an independent Christian media ministry based in Washington, D.C. We are small. We answer to Christ and to the brothers and sisters who walk with us.
