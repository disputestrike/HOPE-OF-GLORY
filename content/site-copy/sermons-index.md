# Sermons

Verse-by-verse teaching from the Bible, in plain English. Each sermon has scripture, written notes, and audio.

We publish new sermons regularly. You can browse by book of the Bible, by topic, or by date. Older sermons stay available — the Word does not expire.

A note: our sermons are drafted with the help of AI tools and then reviewed by a human before publication. See our [AI disclosure](/ai-disclosure) for full detail. If you spot an error in any teaching, please tell us through [corrections](/corrections) or [contact](/contact).

You do not need an account to listen, read, or download.

## Listening for the first time?

If you are new to the Bible or new to Christ, the **Gospel of John** is a good place to start. It tells you who Jesus is in His own words.
