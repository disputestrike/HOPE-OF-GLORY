# Welcome, New Believer

If you have just begun to follow Jesus Christ — or you think you might be beginning — welcome. Heaven rejoices over you, and so do we.

This page is here to help you take the next few steps without feeling lost.

> "I tell you, even so there will be more joy in heaven over one sinner who repents, than over ninety-nine righteous people who need no repentance." — Luke 15:7 (WEB)

---

## You don't need to have it all figured out

You are not behind. You are not unqualified. You do not need a Bible degree, a clean past, or the right vocabulary.

You need Jesus. You have Him.

If you have trusted Christ — believed He is the Son of God, that He died for your sins, and that He rose from the dead — He is yours, and you are His. That is the gospel, and it does not get less true on a hard day.

> "...because, if you will confess with your mouth that Jesus is Lord and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved." — Romans 10:9 (WEB)

---

## A few next steps

**1. Read the Gospel of John.**
Start with the Gospel of John in the New Testament. It tells you who Jesus is in His own words. Read a chapter a day. Do not worry about understanding everything. Read it like a letter from someone who loves you.

**2. Talk to Him.**
Prayer is just talking to God. Out loud, in your head, in writing — He hears. You can start with the simple prayer on our [prayer page](/prayer), or with your own words. He is not grading.

**3. Find a local church.**
This one matters. The Christian life is not a solo activity. You need brothers and sisters. You need to hear scripture preached, sing with others, share the Lord's Supper, and be known.

Look for a church that:

- Teaches the Bible.
- Preaches Jesus Christ crucified and risen.
- Loves people, not power.
- Is honest about money.
- Welcomes questions.

If you would like help finding one near you, [contact us](/contact) and we will do our best to point you to a faithful church in your area.

**4. Get baptized.**
Baptism is how Jesus told His followers to publicly identify with Him. Your local church can help you take that step.

**5. Tell someone.**
You do not have to give a speech. Just tell one person: "I have started to follow Jesus." That sentence is a powerful thing.

---

## Things you don't have to do

You don't have to clean yourself up before God will accept you. He already has.

You don't have to memorize a list of dos and don'ts. The Spirit teaches you over time.

You don't have to be afraid of your past. It is forgiven.

You don't have to perform. Just walk with Him.

---

## What if I'm not sure I really believe yet?

That's okay. Tell Him so. He is not afraid of your doubt. He is patient.

Try this honest prayer:

> *God, if You are real, show me. If Jesus is who He said He is, help me see. I want to know the truth. Amen.*

The Lord answers prayers like that. Not always quickly, but always faithfully.
