# What We Believe

These are the core convictions of Hope of Glory Ministry. They are not new. They are the historic faith of the Christian church, drawn from scripture and confessed by believers across centuries and continents.

We keep this page short on purpose. Many good questions branch off from each point. We are glad to discuss them in the [Ask Hope](/ask) chat or by [contact](/contact).

---

## The Bible

We believe the Bible — the sixty-six books of the Old and New Testaments — is the inspired Word of God, true and trustworthy, and the final authority for what we believe and how we live.

Scripture interprets scripture. We read each passage in its context, in light of the whole.

When we quote the Bible on this site, we use the World English Bible (WEB), the King James Version (KJV), or our own paraphrase, which we mark plainly.

---

## God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three Persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit. One in essence. Three in Person. Equal in glory, power, and love.

This is a mystery the Bible reveals. It is not a contradiction. It is the God who is.

---

## Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God who became truly human without ceasing to be truly God.

He was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the virgin Mary, lived a sinless life, taught the kingdom of God, was crucified under Pontius Pilate, died for our sins, was buried, and rose bodily from the dead on the third day.

He ascended to the right hand of the Father. He is the one Mediator between God and humanity. He will return.

> "...Christ in you, the hope of glory." — Colossians 1:27 (WEB)

---

## The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit is God, fully personal, fully divine. He convicts the world of sin, draws people to Christ, indwells every believer, gives gifts to the church, and produces in His people the fruit of love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control.

---

## Humanity and sin

We believe every human being is made in the image of God and is loved by God.

We also believe every human being — apart from Christ — is fallen, separated from God by sin, and unable to save themselves.

This is not a put-down. It is the honest diagnosis the Bible gives so that the cure can be received.

---

## Salvation by grace through faith

We believe salvation is a gift of God, received by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone. Not earned. Not deserved. Not bought.

> "For by grace you have been saved through faith, and that not of yourselves; it is the gift of God, not of works, that no one would boast." — Ephesians 2:8–9 (WEB)

Faith in Christ leads to a changed life — repentance from sin, love for God and neighbor, and the growing image of Christ in us — but the change is the fruit of salvation, not the price of it.

---

## The Church

We believe the church is the worldwide family of all who trust in Jesus Christ. It is His body and His bride.

The local church — a real congregation that gathers, worships, baptizes, breaks bread, hears the Word, and cares for one another — is essential. Online ministry like ours is a help. It is not a replacement for the local church.

If you do not have a local church, we will gladly help you look. See [New Believers](/new-believers).

---

## The return of Christ

We believe Jesus Christ will return personally, visibly, and gloriously to judge the living and the dead, to make all things new, and to dwell with His people forever.

We do not set dates. We watch, work, and wait.

---
