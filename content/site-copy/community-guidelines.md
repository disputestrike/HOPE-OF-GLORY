# Community Guidelines

> What we expect from one another in this space, and what you can expect from us.

---

Hope of Glory Ministry is a digital space where seekers, new believers, longtime
Christians, doubters, and the suffering can come to ask, learn, pray, and be heard.
For this to remain a place that honors Christ and serves people, we hold the following
guidelines.

## What we welcome

- **Honest questions.** Skeptical, hostile, tender, broken — all welcome.
- **Doubt.** No one is punished here for not yet believing.
- **Disagreement.** Stated charitably.
- **Personal stories.** Yours matter.
- **Real life.** Including the parts you can't say out loud anywhere else.

## What we ask of you

1. **Treat others as image-bearers of God.** Every commenter, questioner, and
   moderator is a person Christ died for.
2. **No slurs, no targeting, no dehumanization.** We compare doctrines, never insult
   people-groups. Whether you're discussing Muslims, atheists, Jewish people, Catholics,
   Orthodox, LGBTQ+ neighbors, JWs, Mormons, or anyone else — speak as if Christ
   is listening, because he is.
2. **No spam or self-promotion.** This is not a marketplace.
3. **No misrepresenting the ministry.** Quote us in context. Disagree with our actual
   positions, not strawmen.
4. **No exploiting the vulnerable.** Do not approach people in crisis flows with sales,
   fundraising, alternative theologies, or scams.
5. **Respect the moderators.** We are a small team. We make mistakes. If we get
   something wrong, write us — patiently — and we will reconsider.

## What we won't do

- We won't bury hard questions in vague platitudes.
- We won't promise outcomes only God can give.
- We won't pretend AI is a pastor.
- We won't take sides on secondary doctrines we are not bound to.
- We won't sell or share your data.
- We won't ask for donations during a prayer or crisis interaction.

## When we remove content

We may remove, hide, or edit content that:

- Threatens harm to a person or group
- Includes slurs, doxxing, or harassment
- Spams, scams, or impersonates someone
- Knowingly spreads false claims about the ministry
- Encourages self-harm, abuse, or illegal activity
- Targets minors with inappropriate material

When we remove content, we tell the author why. When we make a public mistake, we
correct it on the [Corrections](/corrections) page.

## What to do if you have a concern

Reach us at [hello@hopeofglory.ministry](mailto:hello@hopeofglory.ministry) or via the
[contact form](/contact). For doctrinal disputes about a published teaching, please
include the page URL and a specific quote. For safety concerns, see the
[Crisis disclaimer](/crisis-disclaimer) and the [Crisis resources](/help/crisis-resources).

## Final note

This is not a perfect space. We are a young ministry, and we are learning. We invite
patience as you would extend to any neighbor — and we promise to extend the same.

> *Be kind to one another, tenderhearted, forgiving each other, just as God also in
> Christ forgave you.* — Ephesians 4:32 (WEB)
