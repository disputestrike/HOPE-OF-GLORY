# Prayer

You are not alone. If you would like prayer, we would be honored to pray for you.

> "Is any among you suffering? Let him pray. Is any cheerful? Let him sing praises." — James 5:13 (WEB)

---

## Share a request

Use the form below to send a prayer request. It can be a sentence or a page. It can be about anything — a fear, a loss, a hope, a person you love, a decision you are facing, a sin you are wrestling, a thanksgiving.

A human member of the ministry will read your request and pray.

[Open the prayer form →](/prayer/new)

---

## How requests are handled

- **Anonymous is fine.** You do not have to give your name or any identifying detail. A first name, an initial, or nothing at all is welcome.
- **Optional follow-up.** If you give us an email, we may write back with a verse or a short word of encouragement. If you would rather not hear back, leave it blank.
- **Private.** We do not publish, sell, or share your requests. We do not post them on social media. They are not used to train AI systems.
- **Read by a person.** Real people read and pray. AI tools may help us organize requests, but the praying is done by believers.

For full detail, see our [privacy policy](/privacy).

---

## In a crisis

If you are in danger or in a mental health emergency, this form is not the right place. Please call **911** for emergencies or **988** for the U.S. Suicide and Crisis Lifeline. See [Hope Line](/hope-line) for more.

We will still pray for you. But please get real, immediate help first.

## A simple prayer to begin

If you do not know how to start, this is a prayer that has helped many:

> *Lord Jesus, I do not have all the words. I am here. Please meet me. Show me who You are. Forgive what needs forgiving. Heal what needs healing. Lead me one step closer to You today. Amen.*

The Lord hears.
