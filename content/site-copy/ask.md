# Ask Hope

A Bible Q&A chat. Ask an honest question about scripture, faith, or the Christian life, and receive a thoughtful, scripture-grounded answer.

[Open the chat →](/ask/chat)

---

## What Ask Hope is

Ask Hope is an AI assistant designed to point you back to the Bible. It will quote scripture, summarize what historic Christian teaching has said, and try to answer your question with care.

It is good for questions like:

- "What does the Bible say about forgiveness?"
- "Who wrote the book of Hebrews?"
- "What is the gospel, in plain English?"
- "Help me understand Romans 8."
- "How do I start reading the Bible?"

---

## What Ask Hope is not

Ask Hope is **not a pastor**. It cannot shepherd you, sit with you in suffering, or take the place of a local church.

It is **not a counselor, therapist, doctor, or lawyer**. It cannot diagnose or treat anything, and it cannot give legal advice.

It is **not a prophet**. It does not speak new revelation. It points to scripture, which is already complete.

It will sometimes get things wrong. If an answer seems off, check it against the Bible itself, ask a trusted believer, or write to us through [contact](/contact). Real errors get listed on our [corrections page](/corrections).

---

## In a crisis

If you are in danger or in a mental health emergency, please do not use this chat. Call **911** for emergencies or **988** for the U.S. Suicide and Crisis Lifeline. See [Hope Line](/hope-line).
