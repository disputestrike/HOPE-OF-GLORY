# Contact

We are glad you are reaching out.

You can write to us about anything — a question about a sermon, a correction, a story of what the Lord is doing in your life, a request to be removed from our list, or simply hello.

We are a small team. We read every message. We try to reply within a few days.

- **Email:** hello@hopeofglory.ministry
- **Phone (Hope Line):** (202) 555-0100 — see [Hope Line hours](/hope-line)
- **Mail:** Washington, D.C.

**A reminder before you write:** if you are in a medical or safety emergency, please call **911**. If you are in suicidal crisis, please call or text **988**. This contact form is not monitored in real time.

For prayer requests, the [prayer page](/prayer) is the best place to send them.

For Bible questions you want a fast answer to, try [Ask Hope](/ask).
