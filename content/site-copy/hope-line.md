# The Hope Line

A free phone line for conversation, scripture, and prayer.

**Call:** (202) 555-0100

---

## When you can call

The Hope Line is staffed during posted hours, in U.S. Eastern Time. Outside those hours, you will reach a voicemail, and we will return your call when the line opens again.

*(Posted hours will appear here. Check this page before you call.)*

If you would like to be called at a specific time, you can also request a callback through our [contact page](/contact).

---

## What to expect

When you call during open hours, a real person will answer. We are believers — most of us volunteers — and we are glad you called.

We will listen. We will read scripture with you if you would like. We will pray with you. We will not preach at you, and we will not pressure you.

Calls are not recorded. We do not sell, share, or publish what you say.

---

## What the Hope Line is not

**The Hope Line is not an emergency service.** Please read this carefully.

- **If you are in a medical or safety emergency**, please hang up and call **911**.
- **If you are in suicidal crisis or thinking of harming yourself**, please hang up and call or text **988** — the U.S. Suicide and Crisis Lifeline. They are trained, available 24 hours a day, and free.
- **Outside the U.S.**, please call your country's emergency or crisis number.

Our volunteers are not therapists, counselors, doctors, or first responders. They cannot send help, dispatch police, or provide clinical care. We will gladly pray with you while you make the call to the right service, but please make that call.

---

## Topics we are glad to talk about

- A passage of scripture you are trying to understand.
- A prayer you would like prayed.
- Questions about Jesus, the gospel, or starting a life of faith.
- Encouragement when you are weary.
- Finding a faithful local church near you.
- A correction or concern about something we have published.

---

## Topics we cannot help with

- Medical advice.
- Legal advice.
- Mental health treatment.
- Crisis or emergency response.
- Financial counseling.

We will refer you to the right kind of help when we can.

---

## A note about AI

The phone is answered by a human during open hours. We do not currently use AI to answer phone calls. If we ever change that, we will say so here first.

See our full [AI disclosure](/ai-disclosure).
