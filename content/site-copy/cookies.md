# Cookies

> Plain English. What we set, and why.

---

A cookie is a small file your browser stores on your device so a website can remember
you between page loads. We use a small number of cookies, mostly because the site
would not function without them. We don't use them to track you across the web for
advertising.

## What we set

### Strictly necessary (always on)

- **`hog_session`** — used to keep your Ask Hope chat conversation tied to one
  context within a 24-hour window. HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax, secure in production.
- **NextAuth session cookies** — used only for admin sign-in. You will only see
  these if you are an authorized admin. They are not set for visitors.

### Functional (used only if you opt in by submitting a form)

- **No cookies set just for browsing.** We do not set advertising or third-party
  tracking cookies.

### Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting first-party analytics to measure aggregate page
performance. We do not use Google Analytics on this site at launch. If we add
privacy-respecting analytics later, this page will be updated and the disclosure
will be visible.

## What we don't do

- We don't sell your data.
- We don't share your data with advertisers.
- We don't use cookies to follow you around the internet.
- We don't store more information than we need.

## Your choices

- You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Strictly necessary
  cookies will reset on each visit, which may break some functionality (like
  keeping your Ask Hope conversation going).
- You can use the site in private/incognito mode.
- You can request deletion of any data tied to you by emailing
  [hello@hopeofglory.ministry](mailto:hello@hopeofglory.ministry).

## Updates

If we add or change cookies, we will update this page and reflect the change date
at the bottom.
