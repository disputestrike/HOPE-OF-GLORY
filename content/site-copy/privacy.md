# Privacy Policy

We will keep this short and plain. If anything is unclear, please [write to us](/contact) and we will explain in normal words.

**Last updated:** *(date will appear here when the site goes live)*

---

## The short version

- We collect the least information we can.
- You give us your email only if you choose to.
- We never sell, share, or rent your information.
- You can ask us to delete your data at any time.

---

## What we collect

**Email address** — only if you sign up for the Daily Word, send a prayer request, donate, or contact us. We use it to send what you asked for and to reply to you. Nothing else.

**Phone number** — only if you choose to give it to us, for example to request a callback on the Hope Line. We do not share it.

**Prayer requests** — what you write in the prayer form. These are read by people in the ministry and prayed over. They are not published, sold, or used to train AI systems.

**Ask Hope chat messages** — your questions and our answers. These are processed by AI services to generate a response. We do not sell them. We may review them in aggregate, without identifying you, to improve quality and catch errors.

**Donations** — if you give, our payment processor (currently PayPal) collects what it needs to handle the transaction. We see only what they show us — typically your name, email, and amount.

**Basic site analytics** — anonymous data about how many people visit, which pages they read, and roughly where in the world they are. This helps us know what is useful. We do not build profiles on individual readers.

---

## What we don't collect

- We do not require an account to read, listen, or use the chat.
- We do not collect your location beyond a rough country/region.
- We do not track you across other websites.
- We do not sell anything to advertisers.

---

## How we use your information

Only for the reason you gave it to us, and to fulfill our ministry. For example:

- The email you give for the Daily Word is used to send the Daily Word.
- The prayer request you send is read and prayed for.
- The contact message you write is read by a human and replied to.

We will never use your email for something you did not sign up for.

---

## Who sees your information

A small number of people in the ministry, only as needed for the work above. We do not give your information to:

- Advertisers.
- Data brokers.
- Other ministries.
- Anyone for any payment.

We use a few service providers (for email delivery, hosting, payment, and AI processing) who handle data on our behalf, under contract. They are bound to keep it private. We choose providers who respect privacy.

---

## Cookies

We use the minimum cookies needed for the site to work and for basic analytics. We do not use advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser; most of the site will still work.

---

## Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it.

---

## Your choices

You can:

- **Unsubscribe** from any email by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of it.
- **Ask us what we have about you** by emailing **hello@hopeofglory.ministry**.
- **Ask us to delete what we have about you** by emailing the same address. We will do so promptly, unless we are required by law to keep something.

We honor these requests regardless of where you live.

---

## Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure, but we treat what you share with care.

---

## Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a meaningful way, we will post the new version here and update the date at the top. For anyone on our email list, we will send a short note.
