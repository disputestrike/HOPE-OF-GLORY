# Finding a faithful local church

Hope of Glory is not your church. We are a media ministry. We point you to the body of Christ
where you live, because the New Testament knows nothing of disembodied Christians.

This page does not list specific churches by name — we are not in a position to vouch for
every congregation. Instead, here are questions to ask and directories to consult.

## What to look for

A faithful local church will:

- **Teach the Bible carefully.** Sermons take Scripture seriously, not as a launchpad for the
  pastor's opinions.
- **Confess the historic Christian faith.** The Apostles' Creed and the Nicene Creed describe
  the boundary. (See `content/creeds/`.)
- **Preach Christ.** Christ crucified, risen, reigning, returning. The gospel is the center,
  not a footnote.
- **Practice baptism and communion** as Christ commanded.
- **Care for one another.** You should be known and missed.
- **Send people out.** The church exists for the world, not just for itself.

## Questions to ask before you join

1. What do you believe about the Bible? Is it the inspired Word of God, sufficient for faith and
   practice?
2. What do you believe about Jesus Christ — fully God, fully man, crucified, risen, ascended,
   returning?
3. What is your statement of faith? Where can I read it?
4. How do you handle disagreement? With charity?
5. Do you exercise church discipline lovingly when needed?
6. Who is your church accountable to?
7. How do you spend the offerings you receive?

## Directories that take theology seriously

These are not endorsements of every listed church, but they are starting points:

- **The Gospel Coalition — Church Directory** (gospel-centered, broadly Reformed/evangelical)
- **9Marks Healthy Church Directory** (criteria-based, careful)
- **PCA Locator** (Presbyterian Church in America)
- **OPC Directory** (Orthodox Presbyterian Church)
- **SBC Church Finder** (Southern Baptist Convention)
- **LCMS Locator** (Lutheran Church Missouri Synod)
- **Acts 29 Network**
- **Sovereign Grace Churches**

If you cannot find a faithful church near you, write us at contact.

## Red flags

If a church teaches any of these, look elsewhere prayerfully:

- That Jesus is not fully God or fully man
- That salvation is by works or by joining a particular institution
- That money you give will be returned multiplied to you ("seed faith")
- That the pastor is a prophet who must not be questioned
- That women, racial minorities, or LGBTQ+ people are subhuman or unloved by God
- That a particular date for Christ's return has been revealed
- That a modern political figure is the Antichrist
- That you must trust them rather than Scripture
